Utilities and structure

Propane (LP) — 95K BTU, two drops

East wall: Stub-out → under deck → Blackstone 25K + Napoleon IR 10K = 35K BTU.

West wall: Separate drop → Coyote C3PBLP 60K BTU.

Total: 95K BTU. Down from 200K (PRO 825). Traeger + induction = electric.

Water / drain

Through west wall at window center. Hot/cold + drain for Ruvati sink. Scotsman tees off cold. Gravity drain to sink drain. Hose bib on bar east end.

Electrical — 6 dedicated circuits

1. KB 36" — 20A ded. 2. KB 24" — 20A ded. 3. Scotsman — 15A ded. 4. Traeger grill — 120V GFCI ded. 5. Traeger induction — 120V ded. 6. Blackstone — 120V. Plus: Napoleon IR 110V, TV outlet, GFCI work zones, LED strips.

Framing

All treated lumber. Insulating jackets: Blackstone 6228, Coyote CJAKTC1PB. Napoleon IR: cement board heat shield. Traeger: self-contained (verify 18" clearance to combustibles). No steel studs needed.

Post position

SW corner of east wall, 26" inset from deck edge. Through-bolted to stringers. Bar butts in from south. Cooking run uninterrupted 130".

Deck loading

Traeger: 252 lbs. Blackstone: ~150 lbs. KB coolers: ~335 lbs combined. Blocking under all bottom tracks. Sister joists under fridge wall + griddle zone.
