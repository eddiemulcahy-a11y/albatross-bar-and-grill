Appliance install notes — zone by zone (south to north)

Zone 1: Coyote C3PBLP power burner (24"W)

Use & care manual  |  Install guide + jacket dims
Design intent: Power burner for crab boils, stock pots, and high-heat cooking. Positioned next to the sink and trash for a clean prep-cook-clean workflow. Sits above the dog door clearance zone at the south corner of the wall.
Gas: LP 3/8" flare fitting with individual shutoff valve. Separate LP drop on this wall — do NOT tee from east wall cooking run supply. 60K BTU demand. Verify regulator sizing with plumber.
Electrical: 120V outlet for electronic ignition (verify if LED knob is battery or wired per Coyote manual).
Jacket: CJAKTC1PB insulated jacket REQUIRED — treated lumber is combustible. Install jacket per Coyote spec before dropping in burner. Ensure adequate ventilation below. Jacket is 11"H below counter, 24.875"W × 22.75"D.
Dog door clearance: 20" clear from deck surface up. Dog door is in the adjacent 45" SW wall but swings into this zone. Frame the Coyote zone so the bottom of the jacket clears the dog door swing arc (~9" clearance). Verify field clearance before framing.

Zone 2: Napoleon BI-1824-1W trash pull-out (18"W)

Napoleon outdoor kitchen components
Utilities: None — no gas, no electrical, no water.
Install: Full-height pull-out (19.75"W × 21.5"D × ~24"H). Mount per Napoleon spec with included hardware. Frame opening to 19.75"W. Soft-close mechanism is built in. Dual waste bins below + 8" paper towel drawer on top — all part of the single pull-out unit.
Note: Ensure pull-out slides clear the countertop overhang when fully extended. 1.5" overhang should clear — verify in field.

Zone 3: Ruvati RVH8030 sink (30"W) + KRAUS Bolden faucet + BI-3024-2D door

Ruvati install instructions  |  Napoleon BI-3024-2D
Sink: Undermount. Granite/porcelain cutout: 29"W × 21"D. Centered on 79" window — verify center alignment with window before cutting countertop. Sink clips mount from below to countertop underside.
Faucet: KRAUS Bolden KPF-1610SFS — single hole, mount through countertop behind sink basin. Requires 1-3/8" hole.
Plumbing: Hot and cold water supply through house wall — tie into indoor kitchen lines. Standard residential drain through house wall. All supply lines need accessible shutoff valves behind the BI-3024-2D doors. Ice maker water supply: 1/4" OD copper tube, tee off the cold water line with a dedicated shutoff valve. Install inline water filter (1/4") on the ice maker supply line before it reaches the Scotsman — change every 6 months.
Winterization: All outdoor supply lines must have low-point drain valves for winter blowout. Install drain petcocks on both hot and cold lines behind the BI-3024-2D doors — accessible without tools. Ice maker supply line needs its own drain valve at the low point. Sink drain and ice maker gravity drain must be pitched to self-drain (no standing water in lines). Before first freeze each year: shut off supply valves inside the house, open drain petcocks, blow out lines with compressed air, and disconnect the ice maker water supply.
Ice maker drain: 3/4" gravity drain line from Scotsman, routed to the sink drain line via tee. MUST run continuously downhill — no traps, no uphill runs. Verify slope before connecting.
Below-counter door: Napoleon BI-3024-2D 30" double door. Mount with included hardware. Provides clean access to all plumbing connections, shutoff valves, and water filter behind doors.

Zone 4: Scotsman SRCG065A-1SS ice maker (15"W)

Install & user manual  |  Service manual  |  Spec sheet
Electrical: Dedicated 15A / 115V outlet behind unit. Max 126V. GFCI protected per code.
Water supply: 1/4" OD copper tube with accessible shutoff valve. Tees from sink cold water line (see Zone 3 plumbing notes). Inline water filter BEFORE the unit.
Drain: 3/4" gravity drain line. Routes to sink drain via tee. Must run continuously downhill — the Scotsman has NO drain pump (gravity drain model SRCG065A-1SS, not the pump model SRCP065A).
Clearances: Front-venting / air-cooled — can be fully enclosed on sides and rear with 0" clearance. Only the front needs to breathe.
Install: Slides into 15" wide cabinet opening. Self-closing field-reversible door — set hinge direction before final install (right-hinged default). Unit is 33.375"H + 1.625" handle = 35"H total — fits within 40" counter height with ~5" clearance at top for counter slab thickness.
Weight: ~115 lbs empty. Ensure blocking under this zone.
Maintenance access: Water filter (behind unit or on supply line) needs replacement every 6 months. Clean with Scotsman Scale Remover every 6 months. Ensure unit can slide out for service.

Zone 5: Fridge wall overlap + corner (~32")

Construction: Dead space — the 108" fridge wall counter depth (25") crosses into this zone at the NE corner. No appliances, no doors, no drawers. Frame and clad this zone to match the rest of the wall but do not cut any openings. The Kingsbottle KBU56ASD 36" cooler sits on the fridge wall side — its back faces into this overlap zone. Ensure adequate ventilation for the cooler's rear exhaust if the overlap zone fully encloses it.
Corner transition: ~4" radius/filler at the NE corner where the window wall meets the fridge wall. Stone veneer wraps the corner — miter or bullnose the stone at the junction.

Zone 4: Traeger induction cooktop (~13"W) — flush right of Traeger

Traeger induction cooktop guide  |  Installation instructions
Design intent: Counter-mounted induction — searing, sautéing, simmering. Flush against Traeger's right side for a seamless workflow.
Install: For built-in kitchens, the cooktop is removed from the Traeger's side shelf and installed into the countertop. Trim kit includes a 6' extension cord. 3" min from any counter edge. Proper airflow below is critical for cooktop longevity.
Electrical: Dedicated 120V circuit (separate from the pellet grill).
Below-counter clearance: 4.5" minimum below cooktop. Open bay with ventilation — no cabinet door needed. Leave the bay open or use a vented panel to ensure airflow.
Cookware: Cast iron, iron, or ferrous stainless steel only. Flat base, 5.5" min diameter. Not compatible with aluminum, copper, glass, or pottery.
